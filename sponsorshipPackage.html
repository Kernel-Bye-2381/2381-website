THANKS TO ALL OUR SPONSORS!

Empowering the Next Generation of Innovators

Welcome to the sponsorship package for VEX V5 Robotics Team 2381! We are a dedicated group of students passionate about Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM), and we participate in the VEX Robotics Competition. This program provides an invaluable hands-on learning experience, fostering critical thinking, problem-solving, and teamwork skills that are essential for future success.

Corporate sponsorships are vital to our team's success. They help us cover the costs of robot parts, competition fees, travel expenses, and outreach programs. Beyond financial support, a partnership with Team 2381 offers a unique opportunity for your company to invest in the community, inspire young minds, and gain valuable exposure to a highly engaged audience.

This package is designed to outline how a partnership with Team 2381 can be a mutually beneficial and value-added experience. We believe in building long-term relationships with our sponsors, focusing on shared values and demonstrating the tangible impact of your investment.

Why Partner with Team 2381?

Sponsoring our robotics team is more than just a donation; it's an investment in the future workforce and a demonstration of your company's commitment to STEM education and community engagement. Here's what companies like yours look for, and how partnering with us delivers on those expectations:

  • Exposure: Your generosity will be seen by our enthusiastic event attendees, school communities, and online followers. We want to showcase your support where it matters most – in front of your target audience.
  • Relevant Recognition: We go beyond simple logo placement. We offer meaningful ways to highlight your company, ensuring your brand resonates with our audience and aligns with your marketing goals.
  • Data-Driven Impact: We understand the need for measurable results. We commit to providing you with data on our outreach, website traffic, and social media engagement to demonstrate the return on your investment.
  • Exceptional Communication: We value clear and consistent communication. From initial contact to post-event reporting, we will keep you informed every step of the way, making your sponsorship experience seamless and rewarding.

Before asking for your support, we've considered these crucial questions:

  • Audience Alignment: Which local companies are our event attendees and supporters most likely to support? We aim to connect you with an audience that directly benefits your business.
  • Unique Value: What out-of-the-box ideas can we offer that are uniquely valuable to your company? We're open to creative collaborations that go beyond traditional sponsorship perks.
  • Targeted Advertising: Where does your company currently advertise? We can explore opportunities to integrate our thank-you efforts into your existing marketing channels, reaching your audience directly.
  • Demonstrable Impact: What data can we provide to support your investment? We track our website analytics, social media reach, and email list engagement to show you the tangible impact of your support.

Gain prominent exposure in our mass email communications to our subscriber list. We can even offer exclusive sponsorship in individual emails for maximum impact.

Radio Spot Integration

Enhanced Brand Messaging

Custom Recognition Opportunities

Communication & Impact Reporting

Building a strong, lasting relationship with our sponsors is paramount. Our communication strategy ensures transparency, value, and appreciation throughout our partnership.

Before the Ask: Building the Foundation

  • Personal Contact: We initiate contact in person or over the phone to discuss your company's interests and how a partnership with Team 2381 can align with your goals.
  • Thorough Research: We do our homework, understanding your company's values, past sponsorships, and advertising strategies to craft a truly valuable proposal.
  • Permission to Send: We ensure we have your explicit permission to send our proposal, significantly increasing the likelihood of a positive response.

During the Pitch: Clear and Concise

  • Brief and Direct Emails: Our email proposals are concise and to the point, avoiding ambiguity.
  • Professional PDF Attachment: Proposals are attached as easy-to-view PDFs, clearly outlining the value proposition.

After the "Yes!": Over-Communication is Key

  • Clear Next Steps: We clearly communicate what we need from you (logos, messaging, etc.) and by when, making the process easy for you.
  • Online Commitment Form: Our goal is to provide an online commitment form for simple logo uploads and online payments.
  • Dedicated Support: We are always available to answer questions and ensure a wonderful sponsorship experience.

Post-Event Follow-Up: Demonstrating Impact

After each event, we don't just disappear. We provide a comprehensive **Post-Event Impact Report** to demonstrate the valuable return on your investment. This report includes:

  • Overall Event Impact: Key numbers and narratives highlighting the event's success.
  • Outcome of Sponsorship Dollars: How your investment directly contributed to our achievements.
  • Event Highlights & Testimonials: Photos, videos, and feedback from participants and attendees.
  • Social Media & Website Reach: Data on impressions, engagement, and website views your brand received.
  • Future Partnership Opportunities: A look ahead at how we can continue to collaborate.

We will also add your contact to our quarterly email updates to keep you informed about our team's progress and achievements, making future sponsorships easier than ever.
